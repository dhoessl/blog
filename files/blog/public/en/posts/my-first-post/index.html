What can you expect here#

As far as I feel like it, I will write an entry about topics I am working on. I will break down how I use technologies, how I use them and what mistakes I made, so that someone else doesn’t have to solve the same problems.

What do I write about#

Basically, there should be something about the following topics:

  • Python3
  • Ansible
  • Microcontroller projects

It is not excluded that I will also write about other topics that are on my mind.

Inspiration#

Since I keep running into problems that can only be solved by digging together from different sources, I wanted to provide a post providing the solution to these problems.

My thanks go to Syphdias, who showed me Hugo & PaperMod as tools to write down my thoughts.
